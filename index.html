MEET OUR TRAINER & OWNER

Jayden Trudell, from Windsor, Ontario, is a successful boxer who has won gold at the GeeBee Boxing Tournament in Helsinki, Finland, and was awarded Best Brawler in the tournament. He also won gold at the Canadian National Boxing Championship in 2022. In addition to his achievements in the ring, Jayden is the owner of The Doghouse, a gym where he empowers others to reach their fitness goals through personalized training and a supportive environment.
